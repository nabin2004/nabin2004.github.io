I'm an undergraduate researcher working on **low-resource NLP**, **multilingual speech modeling**, and **knowledge-augmented language systems**. My work spans fine-tuning Nepali ASR models at [Wiseyak Inc.](https://wiseyak.com), contributing to [Hugging Face TRL](https://github.com/huggingface/trl), and building logically validated knowledge graphs at [CAIR-Nepal](https://cair-nepal.org).

Currently a Research Intern at the **Center for AI Research Nepal (CAIR)**, where I co-designed [HeritageGraph](https://cair-nepal.org/research/projects/heritagegraph-illuminating-cultural-legacies-throu) — an OWL ontology for Nepal's cultural heritage aligned with CIDOC-CRM, producing 17,894 inferred triples with zero unsatisfiable classes.

Language isn't just a tool for communication. It's how we interrogate our own thoughts — how we challenge what we know to reach what we don't know yet. That's what makes [NLP more than just a subfield of AI]() for me. It's a path to building machines that can [reason](), [reflect](), and maybe even [rethink]().

[AI is the new electricity.]() If not Edison of this new electricity, I would want to become part of the grid at the very least.
