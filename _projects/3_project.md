**HeritageGraph** is an OWL ontology formalizing Nepal's cultural heritage, developed at the **Center for AI Research Nepal (CAIR)** under the supervision of Dr. Tek Raj Chhetri.

### Highlights

- Aligned with **CIDOC-CRM** and validated for logical consistency, achieving full competency question coverage (**32/32**) across structural, ritual, institutional, and Living Goddess domains.
- Cross-standard semantic alignment across **13 vocabularies** including CIDOC-CRM (42 classes, 71 properties) and PROV-O.
- Verified **OWL-RL logical consistency** via deductive closure — 17,894 inferred triples with **zero unsatisfiable classes**.
- Authored **SHACL constraint schemas** for provenance tracking and contributor ingestion, reducing data integrity violations by ~30%.

Related publication: *"Knowledge Graphs: Structure, Function, and Significance"* — Book Chapter 3, under review at **Elsevier**.
